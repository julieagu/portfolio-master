TALI

TALI is a journaling ecosystem designed to help teens aged 13-16 track and reflect on their emotions, unpack experiences, and learn about intimacy, all in a safe, approachable, and judgment-free space. Rather than offering rigid health content, TALI focuses on building emotional literacy as the foundation for healthy relationships and self-advocacy
